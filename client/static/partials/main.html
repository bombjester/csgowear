Float Value. What is it?

Float values are numbers that represent the condition the a skin. The lower the value, the better or cleaner the skin. The higher the value, the more wear and tear of the skin. The float value of skins are organized by 5 categories. If a skin's float value is between 0.44 – 1.00, it is considered Battle-Scarred. If a skin has a float value of .45, it is considered Battle-Scarred, but the actual paint on the skin may be similar to a Well-Worn skin.

Here are the 5 categories:

  • 0.44 - 1.00 = Battle-Scarred
  • 0.37 - .44 = Well-Worn
  • 0.15 - 0.37 = Field Tested
  • 0.07 - 0.15 = Minimal Wear
  • 0.00 - 0.07 = Factory New
