Float Value. What is it?

Float values are numbers that represent the condition the a skin. The lower the value, the better or cleaner the skin. The higher the value, the more wear and tear of the skin. The float value of skins are organized by 5 categories. If a skin's float value is between 0.44 â€“ 1.00, it is considered Battle-Scarred. If a skin has a float value of .45, it is considered Battle-Scarred, but the actual paint on the skin may be similar to a Well-Worn skin.

Here are the 5 categories:

  • 0.44 - 1.00 = Battle-Scarred
  • 0.37 - 0.44 = Well-Worn
  • 0.15 - 0.37 = Field Tested
  • 0.07 - 0.15 = Minimal Wear
  • 0.00 - 0.07 = Factory New

How to set your steam profile to public

1. Log into your Steam Community Profile.

2. Click the Edit my Profile link under Actions.

3. Click the Settings tab.

4. Choose your privacy state.

5. Click the Save button.
